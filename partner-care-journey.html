The End-to-End Flow

From first contact to lifelong support

Five interconnected stages — each one feeding the next. The earlier stages do the intelligence work so that by the time a member reaches their care manager, the gap is already bridged.

Stage 01
📡
Reach & Recruit
"We find the right members at the right moment"
  • In-app intercept — triggered after a meaningful action, not at random
  • Targeted campaign outreach coordinated with partner comms
  • Care manager referral for power users and edge cases
  • Screener criteria ensure participants reflect real member population
Stage 02
🎙️
Listen & Learn
"Real voices shape every design decision"
  • Moderated sessions via User Interviews — 8–10 per partner
  • Unmoderated validation at scale — 50+ participants
  • Partner holds a silent observer seat in every session
  • Fully PHI/PII safe — synthetic data, screened consent
Stage 03
⚙️
Build Intelligence
"The system learns. The experience adapts."
  • Insights drive adaptive recommendation engine — articles, services, products
  • Assessment answers generate a personalised action plan
  • Recommendations refine continuously with each member interaction
  • Design decisions logged from insight → shipped feature
Stage 04
🤝
Bridge the Gap
"First contact. Already connected."
  • Care manager enters with full member context already built
  • Personalised action plan pre-generated before first call
  • Recommendations already surfaced and waiting to discuss
  • First session is about deepening — not starting from zero
Stage 05
🌿
The Independence Journey
"We walk alongside every stage of life"
  • Ongoing support evolves as member's life stage changes
  • Day 7, 30, and 90 touchpoints keep the experience adaptive
  • Care manager becomes a trusted long-term guide
  • Outcomes loop back to improve the system for every member
How We Build Understanding

Research doesn't end. It compounds.

User testing isn't a one-time gate before launch. It feeds a continuous loop that makes every future member's experience smarter than the last.

1
Test with real members
Moderated + unmoderated sessions
2
Synthesise insights
Affinity mapping → insight statements
3
Adapt the system
Recommendations engine updated
4
Equip the care manager
Richer context at every touchpoint
5
Member feels understood
Better outcomes → more signal

Each cycle closes the gap further between what a member needs and what the care manager already knows.

The Care Manager Advantage

Changing what "first contact" means

Most care models start cold. Ours starts warm — because the work has already been done before the care manager ever picks up the phone.

Without this approach
The care manager starts from zero
✕
First session spent gathering basic information the member has already provided elsewhere
✕
No insight into what the member actually cares about or struggles with
✕
Generic opening recommendations that may not feel relevant
✕
Trust must be built entirely through conversation — takes multiple sessions
The member has to re-introduce themselves. The care manager has to prove their value. Time and trust are both lost.
With our approach
The care manager arrives already equipped
✓
Member's completed assessment and action plan are waiting before the first call
✓
Adaptive recommendations already surfaced — care manager can endorse, adjust, deepen
✓
System has already identified priorities — care manager focuses on relationship, not intake
✓
Member feels recognised immediately — trust accelerates from minute one
"The first call isn't the beginning of the relationship. It's already several steps in."
Post-Retirement Life Stages

We meet members wherever they are

Post-retirement is not a single moment — it's a long arc of changing needs, priorities, and challenges. We design for the full journey, not just the entry point.

🌅
Stage I
Active Independence
Early retirement. Members are active, curious, and navigating a new identity beyond work. They want to plan well, stay healthy, and stay connected.
Wellness planning Financial confidence Purpose & activity Social connection
🧭
Stage II
Navigating Transitions
Mid-retirement. Life brings change — health shifts, family roles evolve, routines adjust. Members need a trusted guide more than information.
Health management Caregiver support Life adjustments Medication guidance
🏡
Stage III
Supported Living
Later retirement. Independence remains central but support becomes essential. Members and families need coordinated, compassionate guidance.
Care coordination Family communication Home & safety Dignity-first care
💛
Stage IV
Legacy & Continuity
A stage often overlooked. Members focus on what they leave behind — relationships, values, and wishes. Our care manager is there for this too.
End-of-life planning Family wishes Memory & legacy Ongoing support
